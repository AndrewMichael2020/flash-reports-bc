Executive Summary

The Architecture of Truth

US interrogation is undergoing a fundamental epistemological transformation. The field is pivoting from an Accusatory Model (intuition-based pressure) to an Information-Gathering Model (science-based intelligence).

This shift is driven by the alarming prevalence of false confessions and the rigorous scientific critique of traditional deception cues. The modern investigator must now operate as a "Cognitive Manager" within a "Digital Siege".

"The goal has shifted from 'getting the guy to talk' to 'getting the guy to talk in a way that stands up in court'."
